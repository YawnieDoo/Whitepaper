Governance

Yawnie Doo will implement a community-driven governance model, allowing token holders to vote on key decisions related to the project’s future. This includes proposals on:

  • Development priorities and feature updates
  • Community initiatives and partnerships
  • Fund allocation from community wallets
  • Future roadmap phases and token utility expansions

Governance will evolve gradually, starting with team-led proposals and eventually transitioning to decentralized decision-making through Yawnie Doo DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization). This ensures that as the project grows, power and influence shift naturally to the community.
